## Resolution

In this repository, I am documenting my progress of learning two new skills: hardware design and CAD. I will hopefully use these in the future to make some really cool projects!

This is made possible by Hack Club's Resolution, a program for teenagers 13-18 years old to learn new skills and earn prizes.

### How it works

I am following the pathways "Hardware" and "Design". These are divided into different weeks: week 1-8. The pathways and weeks have their separate folders with a README to show what the projects are and how I did it, and the project files.
